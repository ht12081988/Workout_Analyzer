CONCEPT OVERVIEW


The AI Workout Form Analyzer is an intelligent, camera-based system that uses computer vision and machine learning to analyze human posture and movement during physical exercises. By leveraging pose estimation models such as Google MediaPipe, the system detects body keypoints and evaluates exercise execution in real time or through recorded video analysis.

The system can:

Track repetitions and range of motion

Detect unsafe or incorrect posture

Provide immediate corrective feedback or detailed post-session reports

The solution can be designed to operate across multiple environments, including home workouts, physiotherapy and rehabilitation programs, gyms, and sports coaching institutes, offering scalable and consistent coaching without continuous human supervision.



KEY FEATURES

Movement Tracking

Uses camera input from  web devices

Detects human joints and body landmarks

Tracks movement frame-by-frame for accuracy

Supports both rule-based classification

Adaptable to new exercises through configuration or training

Form \& Posture Analysis

Calculates joint angles (knee, hip, shoulder, neck)

Monitors alignment (e.g., knee-to-foot, spine posture)

Evaluates range of motion and movement symmetry

Repetition Counting \& Quality Scoring

Counts repetitions accurately using movement thresholds

Assigns form correctness scores per repetition

Highlights deviations from ideal movement patterns

Real-Time \& Post-Workout Feedback

Live visual cues or voice alerts during workouts

Post-session reports summarizing performance

Actionable suggestions for improvement

Analytics \& Reporting

Session-level and historical performance tracking

Trend analysis for improvement or regression
